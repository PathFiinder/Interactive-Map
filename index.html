To select the countries you have visited you can choose the country directly from the map (by clicking on it) or use search box in two different way: by entering the two-letter coutry code (ISO alpha-2) or by entering the country name with the selected option "Country Name"

Selected countries are displayed both in the table divided into continents (sorted by area they occupy) and higlihted on the map.

To remove the country from the list click the trash icon next to the country name or click on selected country directly on map

To diplay the list of all available countries with codes click on icon (bars) next to the button "Click for instructions"
